    Clang: Don't Touch My Code!

    January 24, 2020
    system
    clang

    Clang/LLVM is a toolchain that is widely adopted by both researchers and the industry. Based on LLVM, compilers for new targets are built, and program analysis solutions are developed, thanks to the well-defined LLVM IR. Nevertheless, Clang/LLVM is also famous for some of its strange (often just “different” from gcc) behaviors and very aggressive optimizations.
